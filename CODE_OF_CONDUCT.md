# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

Expected behavior:
- Be constructive and professional
- Assume good intent
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and discussions
that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report issues privately to maintainers through the repository contact channel.
