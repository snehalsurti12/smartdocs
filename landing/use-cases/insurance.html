Why insurance carriers choose SmartDocs

Mass Generation

Renewal season? Generate notices for 50,000 policyholders. Async batch jobs with progress tracking and per-record error handling.

Multi-Line Support

One template per document type, works across auto, home, life, and commercial lines. Data binding handles the differences.

PII-Safe Processing

SSNs, policy numbers, and claims data tagged as PII. Never logged, never cached, never retained after render.

Agent Portal Integration

API-first design integrates with Applied Epic, Vertafore, Duck Creek, or custom agent portals.

Regulatory Compliance

Version-controlled templates ensure every policyholder gets the approved language. Audit trail for DOI examinations.

Bring Your Own Storage

Generated documents go directly to your S3 or Azure bucket. SmartDocs never stores policyholder documents.
