Why healthcare teams choose SmartDocs

PII Field Tagging

Tag fields as name, DOB, SSN, MRN, or medical record. Tagged fields are masked in all logs and excluded from error messages.

Zero Data Retention

Patient data exists only in memory during render. No disk writes, no cache, no temp files. Destroyed after PDF is returned.

Render Isolation

Each document renders in its own browser context, destroyed after use. No data leakage between patient documents.

Bring Your Own Storage

PDFs go to your HIPAA-compliant S3/GCS/Azure bucket. SmartDocs never stores patient documents.

EHR Integration

API-first design works with Epic, Cerner, Athenahealth — any EHR that can make HTTP calls or trigger webhooks.

Batch Processing

Generate EOBs or lab reports for thousands of patients. Async jobs with progress tracking and per-record error reporting.
