Why financial teams choose SmartDocs

Auto-Paginating Tables

Hundreds of transactions flow across pages automatically. Running totals, page numbers, and headers carry through.

Charts & Visualizations

Bar, line, pie, doughnut charts — all SVG-based, all data-bound. Spending breakdowns, portfolio allocation, trend analysis.

Batch Generation

Generate statements for 10,000 accounts in a single job. Async processing with status polling. No timeout limits.

PII Protection

Account numbers, SSNs, and balances tagged as PII. Masked in logs, excluded from error messages, never cached.

Compliance-Ready Audit Trail

Every render logged with template version, timestamp, and tenant. Meet SOX, PCI-DSS, and regulatory requirements.

Verification Codes

QR codes and barcodes for document verification. Real Code 128 encoding. Scannable from any device.
